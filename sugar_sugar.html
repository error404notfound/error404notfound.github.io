Collecting and blood glucose data can be difficult. Sugar Sugar aims to make the process easier.

How To Use Sugar Sugar

Simply take your blood sugar level as you normal would

With Bixby enabled you can say "Add new entry" you will be prompted for your level and a quick diary entry, thats all! Your Level is automatically stored for later retirval..

Search

You can look through your entrys by simply saying "Show me my entries". Sugar Sugar will retirve all your entries.

You can also search for entries that contain a certina word for example "Show me entires with tired" , The relevant search results will be displayed.

Share

Managing data is one of the toughest problems in health care, With Sugar Sugar you are in control, your data is not locked to your device.

You can share your data by selecting a contact you want to send it to. Sugar Sugar composes a spread sheet in CSV format ( wich is compataible with many anyalsis programs ) and emails it directly to a contact you provide.

Just say "Send my data" and you will be guided through the process
